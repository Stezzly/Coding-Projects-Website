Welcome to Blender Project 3 - Creating a Stylized Sword

This project will guide you through the process of creating a stylized sword suitable for video games or animations using Blender. This project is perfect for those who want to explore more artistic aspects of 3D modeling.

Hardware Setup

Ensure you have a capable setup as detailed in previous projects.

Software Requirements

Ensure you have the latest version of Blender installed on your computer, available for free from the Blender Foundation website.

Knowledge Prerequisites

To successfully complete this project, you should be familiar with basic modeling techniques and how to apply simple materials and textures.

Difficulty Rating

Easy Difficulty

Project Time!!!

0%



Project Steps:

  1. Sketch out a design for your sword on paper or using digital software to serve as a reference.
  2. Open Blender and create a new file.

  1. Create a new plane and align it to the front view. Enter edit mode.
  2. Extrude and shape the plane to form the outline of the sword's blade.

  1. Create a cylinder for the handle and position it at the base of the blade.
  2. Modify the cylinder's topology to fit the handgrip and add details like a pommel and guard.

  1. Apply different materials to the blade and handle to differentiate the textures.
  2. Adjust the specular and roughness settings to achieve a metallic look for the blade and a leather or wood texture for the handle.

  1. Set up lighting around the sword to highlight its features.
  2. Render the final image with appropriate resolution and sampling settings for the best quality.

Congratulations, you have created your own stylized sword!
